My Journey

Technology, education, and real human value.

I am a Teaching Assistant at British University Vietnam, a Computer Science graduate, and an MBA holder. I support students academically and personally while building practical systems that improve learning outcomes.

My background combines technical delivery with educational impact. From enterprise software exposure to student-focused projects, I have learned to balance detail-oriented execution with big-picture thinking.

At BUV, I have contributed to events, hackathons, workshops, and showcase activities. These experiences strengthened my communication, mentoring, and cross-functional collaboration skills.

Outside work, I co-founded a football community that brings people together in Hanoi's international alumni network. I also enjoy journaling, content creation, and mentoring.

I care about clear communication, continuous learning, and using technology to make processes more meaningful for people.
